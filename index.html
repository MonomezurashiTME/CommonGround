01 — The Problem

Empty stores,
hungry consumers.

Independent makers are talented, prolific, and invisible. Traditional retail requires volume and margin most can't sustain. Markets are inconsistent. Online channels are algorithmically brutal. The infrastructure works against the SME.

of Australian businesses operate as sole traders
3 in 4

60%

Wholesale is out of reach
Traditional wholesale demands minimum order quantities and margins that most independent makers simply can't meet at small scale.
Markets aren't a business model
Weekend markets require physical presence every week. They're inconsistent, exhausting, and offer no path to stable retail income.
Online channels are saturated
Etsy, Instagram, and direct-to-consumer platforms reward advertising spend over craft quality. Discovery is broken for the independent maker.

Why join us

Built for makers
who are done doing it alone.

Retail without the rent shock
A permanent shopfront has always been out of reach for independent makers. Common Ground divides that cost across the collective — so you get the shelf without the lease.
Reclaim your weekends
No more setting up at 6am. No more packing down in the rain. Our permanent, staffed location means your products are selling while you're making — or resting.
A community that gets it
Every maker in the collective knows what it's like to do this on their own. Common Ground is a room full of people who actually understand your business — and want to see it grow.
Marketing that moves the whole
We invest in the brand collectively. Every campaign, every event, every social post lifts all makers — not just the ones who can afford to advertise individually.
Credibility by association
Being selected for Common Ground signals quality. Our curation standard is the badge — consumers walk in already trusting what's on the shelf.
Stable, predictable income
Unlike markets and seasonal fairs, a permanent retail presence means consistent sales data, repeat customers, and a revenue stream you can actually plan around.
